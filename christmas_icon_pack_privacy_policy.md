# Privacy Policy for Christmas Icon Pack

**Last updated: November 4, 2024**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Christmas Icon Pack ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Information We Collect

**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### Information Accessed Locally

The App accesses the following information solely on your device for functionality purposes:

1. **Installed Applications List**: The App reads the list of applications installed on your device to:
   - Display which apps have available themed icons
   - Show installed vs. not installed apps in the icon preview
   - Detect which launcher apps are installed on your device

2. **Launcher Information**: The App queries for installed launcher applications to:
   - Provide instructions for applying the icon pack
   - Open launcher settings when requested by the user

This information is **only processed locally on your device** and is **never transmitted** to our servers or any third parties.

## Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions:

### Required Permissions

- **INTERNET**: This permission exists in the manifest but is not actively used for data collection or transmission. It may be used for future features like checking for updates.

- **SET_WALLPAPER**: Allows the App to apply Christmas-themed wallpapers to your device when you tap on a wallpaper in the app.

- **QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES** (Package Visibility): Allows the App to detect:
  - Which apps you have installed (to show themed icons for installed apps)
  - Which launcher apps are available on your device

## How We Use Information

All information accessed by the App is used exclusively for:
- Displaying relevant themed icons for your installed applications
- Showing you which launchers are installed on your device
- Providing instructions for applying the icon pack to your launcher
- Applying wallpapers when requested

**We do not:**
- Collect or store any personal data
- Track your usage or behavior
- Share any information with third parties
- Use analytics or advertising services
- Transmit data over the internet

## Data Storage

The App does not store any personal data. All functionality is performed in real-time on your device.
