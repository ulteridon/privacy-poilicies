# Privacy Policy for Battery Animation

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Battery Animation ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Animation Preferences:** We store your selected charging animation preference locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
- **In-App Purchase Data:** When you make a purchase to remove ads, transaction information is processed through Google Play Store. We do not directly collect or store payment information.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** We collect basic device information including:
  - Android version
  - Device model
  - Screen size
  - Battery status (for animation functionality)
- **App Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the app, including:
  - Selected animations
  - App launch frequency
  - Feature usage

### Advertising Information
Our app displays banner advertisements through third-party advertising partners. These partners may collect:
- **Advertising ID:** Your device's advertising identifier
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and version
- **Usage Data:** App interactions and ad performance metrics
- **Location Data (if permitted):** Approximate location for targeted advertising

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **App Functionality:**
   - Display your selected charging animation on lock screen
   - Monitor battery charging status
   - Persist your preferences across app sessions

2. **Advertising:**
   - Display relevant banner advertisements
   - Measure ad performance and engagement
   - Improve ad targeting (through our advertising partners)

3. **In-App Purchases:**
   - Process your purchase to remove advertisements
   - Verify purchase status
   - Restore purchases on reinstallation

4. **App Improvement:**
   - Analyze app usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** Animation preferences are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
- **Security:** We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
- **Data Retention:** Local preferences remain on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

## Permissions We Require

Our app requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Start monitoring service after device reboot |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | Wake screen when charging detected |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Run background service to monitor charging |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE` | Maintain charging monitor service (Android 14+) |
| `SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW` | Display animation over lock screen |
| `USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT` | Launch full-screen animation on lock screen |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Show charging notifications (Android 13+) |
| `INTERNET` | Display advertisements |
| `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | Check network connectivity for ads |

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### General Rights
- **Access:** Request access to your personal data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of targeted advertising

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- **Delete App Data:** Go to Settings > Apps > Battery Animation > Storage > Clear Data
- **Opt-Out of Personalized Ads:**
  - Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  - Or reset your Advertising ID
- **Remove Ads:** Purchase the ad-free version through in-app purchase
