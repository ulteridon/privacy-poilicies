# Privacy Policy for Mirage

**Last Updated:** March 29, 2026

## Introduction

Mirage ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Mirage (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

**Mirage is intended for adults aged 18 and over only.** By using Mirage, you confirm that you are at least 18 years of age and agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Profile Information

During onboarding, you provide the following information to personalize your experience:

- **Name** – The name you enter to be addressed by
- **Age** – Used to verify you meet the 18+ age requirement
- **Gender** – Used to personalize character interactions
- **Preference** – Used to tailor how Mirage presents characters to you

This profile information is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage and is not transmitted to our servers except as described in Section 1.3.

### 1.2 Chat History

- All conversations with AI characters are stored **locally on your device** only
- Chat history is saved per character using AsyncStorage
- We do not back up or transmit your chat history to our servers independently
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored chat data

### 1.3 Chat Messages Sent to Our Server

**When you send a message in a chat, the following information is transmitted to our backend proxy server to generate AI responses:**

- Your chat message content
- Your conversation history (for context)
- Your profile context: name, age, and gender

This data is sent to our proxy server, which processes it using an AI language model to generate responses. This data is used solely to produce your AI companion's reply and is **not retained or stored on our servers** beyond the duration of the API request.

### 1.4 In-App Purchase Information

- Mirage offers a subscription ("mirage_monthly") via In-App Purchases
- Purchases are processed entirely through your device's platform store (Google Play or Apple App Store)
- We do not collect or store your payment card details
- Purchase verification is handled by the platform's native in-app purchase system
- Platform stores may collect payment and purchase data subject to their own privacy policies

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Profile Data
- To personalize AI character interactions and responses

### 2.2 Chat Messages
- To generate AI companion responses via our proxy server

### 2.3 In-App Purchases

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 AI Processing Server

**Chat messages and user profile context are transmitted to our proxy server**, which forwards them to a third-party AI model provider for response generation. This is necessary for the core functionality of the app. The data is processed in real time and is not stored beyond the request.

### 3.2 Platform Stores

When you make in-app purchases, your transaction data is handled by Google Play or the Apple App Store. Their respective privacy policies govern that data.

### 3.3 We Do NOT

- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data for advertising purposes
- Store your chat history on our servers
- Use your data to train AI models

### 3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Investigation of fraud or security issues

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

Profile data and chat history are stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security. We recommend using device encryption and screen lock for additional protection.

### 4.2 Data in Transit

Chat messages and user context are transmitted to our proxy server over HTTPS (encrypted). We use an application-level secret key to authenticate requests to our server.

### 4.3 Limitations

- No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure
- We cannot guarantee absolute security of data in transit or at rest
- You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Profile Data

- You can reset your profile at any time through the Profile screen
- Resetting your profile deletes locally stored profile data and returns you to onboarding
- Profile data included in past chat API requests cannot be retroactively deleted from server logs, if any exist

### 5.2 Chat History

- All chat history is stored locally on your device
- You can clear chat history by uninstalling the app
- Individual chat clearing may be available within the app

### 5.3 Data Deletion

- Uninstalling Mirage permanently removes all locally stored data (profile, chat history)
- Data transmitted to our server during chat sessions is not retained after the response is delivered
