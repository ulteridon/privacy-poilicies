## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often does SayTime announce the time?

You choose. Open the Settings screen and pick an interval — every 5, 10, 15, 30, or 60 minutes. SayTime will announce at that cadence as long as the app is running.

### Will SayTime speak while I'm listening to music or a podcast?

Yes. Other audio is briefly lowered ("ducked") while SayTime speaks, then returns to full volume once the announcement finishes. This works with Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, podcasts, and most audiobook apps.

### Can I stop announcements at night?

Yes. Turn on **Silent Hours** in Settings and pick a time range (for example, 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM). SayTime will stay quiet during that window and resume automatically afterward.

### Does SayTime work when the screen is locked?

Yes. SayTime runs in the background and will continue to announce the time even while your phone is locked or you're using other apps. If you force-quit the app (swipe it away in the app switcher), announcements will stop until you reopen it.

### The voice sounds robotic — can I change it?

SayTime uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine. You can pick language (English, Hindi, Spanish, French) and a male or female voice inside the app. For higher-quality voices on iOS, go to **Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices** and download a premium or enhanced voice for your language — SayTime will automatically use it.

### Announcements aren't firing when the app is in the background. What do I do?

A few things to try:

- Make sure SayTime is running (not force-quit from the app switcher).
- Check that **Silent Hours** isn't currently active.
- On iOS, confirm the Ringer/Silent switch on the side of your phone isn't set to silent — this can block some audio.
- Confirm your phone's volume is turned up.
- If you have Low Power Mode or Battery Saver enabled, background activity may be limited. Try turning it off temporarily to test.

If announcements still don't fire, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll dig in.

### SayTime is ducking my music but the music never comes back to full volume.

Please update to the latest version — this was addressed in a recent release. If it's still happening on the latest version, email us the app version number (shown on the About screen) and what app you were using for music.

### Can I change the clock face?

Yes. Open Settings → Clock Face, and pick from Old Clock, Milky Way, Minimalist, Timeless Treasure, Coder, or Royal Rome.

### How do I reset SayTime to its default settings?

Delete and reinstall the app. All settings are stored on your device only, so uninstalling wipes everything clean.

### Does SayTime collect any data about me?

No. SayTime stores all settings locally on your device and does not transmit any data to our servers. See the [Privacy Policy](policy.html?file=talkingClock_privacy_policy.md) for details.

### Can I get a refund for an in-app purchase?

In-app purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) and sign in with the Apple ID used to make the purchase.

## Reporting a Bug

When reporting a bug, including the following information helps us fix it faster:

- Device model (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro)
- iOS version (Settings → General → About → iOS Version)
- SayTime version (About screen inside the app)
- What you were doing when the issue happened
- What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
- A screen recording, if possible
