Mirage – Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Data

All data in Mirage is stored locally on your device. No personal data is stored on external servers.

To delete all your data:

  1. Open the Mirage app
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap Reset Profile & Data
  4. Confirm by tapping Reset

This immediately and permanently deletes:

  • Your profile (name, age, gender, preference)
  • All chat histories with every character
  • All roleplay session histories

What Is Kept

Nothing. All Mirage data is removed from your device instantly.

Third-Party Services

Chat messages are processed by xAI (Grok API) to generate responses. Mirage does not retain these messages. For xAI's data practices, refer to xAI's privacy policy.
