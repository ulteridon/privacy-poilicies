# Privacy Policy for Manjhari

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Manjhari ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **App Preferences:** We store your selected preferences (such as audio loop settings or volume) locally on your device using `SharedPreferences`.
- **Audio Files:** If you use the file picker to select custom audio files, the app only accesses those specific files for playback purposes. We do not upload or transmit your files or any data associated with them.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **App Usage Data:** We do not automatically collect personal information or track your usage across other apps or websites. The app may access basic system information required for stability and performance.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **App Functionality:**
   - Play audio files selected by you or provided by the app.
   - Maintain background playback and foreground services for an uninterrupted experience.
   - Persist your preferences across app sessions.

## Third-Party Services

Manjhari is designed with privacy in mind. We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

### Google Play Services
Our app use Google Play Services for distribution and updates through the Google Play Store. You can review their privacy policy here: [Google Play Services Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** All app preferences and local file paths are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.
- **Security:** We prioritize the security of your data and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it.

## Permissions We Require

Our app requires the following permissions to function correctly:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Run background service for continuous audio playback. |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK` | Specifically for media playback in the background (Android 14+). |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | Prevent device from sleeping during audio playback to ensure a smooth experience. |

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is not directed specifically at children, though it is suitable for all ages.
